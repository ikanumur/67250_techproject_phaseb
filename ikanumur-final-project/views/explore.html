About the museum

What MonoMuse Is

  • Museum Overview: MonoMuse is a contemporary museum focused on art, technology, and creativity.
  • Who It Serves: Students, families, artists, designers, tourists, and the wider community.
  • Mission: To inspire curiosity through immersive and accessible museum experiences.
  • Founded: 2021
  • Milestones: Expanded digital exhibitions, visitor-centered planning, and immersive programming.
