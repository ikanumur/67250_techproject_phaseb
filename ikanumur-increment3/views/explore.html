Explore

MonoMuse is a contemporary museum centered on the relationship between art, technology, and human creativity. Through immersive exhibitions and digital installations, the museum invites visitors to explore how innovation shapes culture, design, and everyday life.

  • Mission Statement: To inspire curiosity by connecting art, technology, and creativity through interactive museum experiences.
  • Who the Museum Serves: Students, families, artists, designers, technologists, and members of the broader community.
  • Founding Year: 2021
  • Milestones:
    • 2021: MonoMuse opened its first digital gallery.
    • 2022: Launched interactive installations for student visitors.
    • 2024: Expanded with a new immersive media exhibition space.
